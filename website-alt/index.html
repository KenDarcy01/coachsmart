Everything a GAA coach needs.

Six tools, built specifically for the demands of GAA coaching — from Tuesday training in the rain to the championship final.

01
Event Scheduling

Schedule training, matches, and team meetings in seconds. Players get push notifications and can confirm availability with one tap. No more chasing.

Squad Management
